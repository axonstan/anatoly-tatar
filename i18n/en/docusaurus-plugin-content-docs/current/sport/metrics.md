# Metrics

- SHR - Shoulder-to-Hip Ratio
- CWR - Chest-to-Waist Ratio
- BFP - Body Fat Percent

**Shoulder Width**:

1. acromion to acromion

**Hip Width**:

1. iliac crests, widest horizontal distance across them

**Chest Circumference**:

1. wrap tape around chest at the nipple line

**Waist circumference**:

1. wrap tape around the narrowest point of your torso
